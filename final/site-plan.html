LasaPH: Discover the Flavors of Filipino Cuisine

Website Planning Document

1. Site Name

Site Name: LasaPH

Reason: The word “Lasa” means “taste” in Filipino. LasaPH captures the essence of Filipino cuisine — flavorful, cultural, and proudly Filipino.

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to celebrate and promote Filipino food culture. It provides:

  • Information about iconic Filipino dishes.
  • A dynamic page featuring popular foods with origins and ingredients.
  • A simple form for users to share or subscribe to Filipino food updates.

3. Scenarios

  • “What are the most famous traditional Filipino dishes I should try?”
  • “Can I share my own recipe or get updates about Filipino food culture?”

4. Color Schema

These colors were chosen to represent warmth, culture, and the natural tones of Filipino food:

  • Primary: #A12525 – Deep Red (used for headers and accents)
  • Secondary: #F3E5AB – Warm Beige (used for backgrounds)
  • Accent: #1E3A8A – Navy Blue (used for buttons and links)

5. Typography

  • Headings: Poppins, sans-serif — bold, modern, and clean for clarity.
  • Body Text: Merriweather, serif — adds a touch of traditional elegance.
